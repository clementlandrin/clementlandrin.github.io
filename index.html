PBR & Toon shading

During my school program at Télécom Paris, I developped a 3D rendering app using OpenGL 4. Some of the key rendering features have been implemented such as Physically-Based-Rendering (PBR), toon shading, simplification, filtering, texturing and normal mapping.

Particular attention have been paid to the implemention of a research paper on X-Toon shading written by Pascal Barla, Joëlle Thollot and Lee Markosian. X-toon shading is basically a toon shader using a 2D texture instead of 1D texture and criteria such as distance from the camera.

Work In Progress

During this last year in engineering school, I'm doing a Master Research and Innovation Project, called PRIM in French. This project consists in the analyze, the implementation and the improvment of a research paper. I'm working on the paper Lighting Grid Hierarchy for Self-illuminating Explosions written by Cem Yuksel and Can Yuksel.

This includes the resolution of Navier-Stokes using both vortex particle method and vorticity confinement as described in the paper A Vortex Particle Method for Smoke, Water and Explosions and the implementation of self-illumination over a multi-grid hierarchy.

I want this project to be an opportunity to improve both my OpenGL skills and my ability to resolve complex optimization problems such as this one.
