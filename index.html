Work In Progress

During this last year at engineering school, I'm doing a Master Research and Innovation Project, called PRIM in French. This project consists of the analysis, the implementation and the improvement of the paper Lighting Grid Hierarchy for Self-illuminating Explosions written by Cem Yuksel and Can Yuksel.

This includes the resolution of Navier-Stokes's equation (a complex equation in fluid mechanics) as described in the paper A Vortex Particle Method for Smoke, Water and Explosions. Then, each particle of the fluid has to illuminate the scene to correctly render explosions.

This project is an opportunity to improve both my OpenGL skills and my ability to resolve complex optimization problems.
